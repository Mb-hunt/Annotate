AI PERFORMANCE - WHAT TO EVALUATE:

When evaluating AI Performance, keep an eye on these four behaviors:
1. Navigation: Was the path logical and direct, or did it meander to irrelevant pages?
2. Interaction: Did it click the correct buttons and links precisely?
3. Efficiency: Were there wasted steps, loops, or unnecessary actions?
4. Output Quality: Is the final scraped text clean and well-formatted?

Scale (Use EXACT WORDS):
- Excellent (expert and optimal)
- Good (effective but with minor flaws)
- Acceptable (clumsy but functional)
- Poor (illogical or flawed)
- Unacceptable (broken or nonsensical)

------------------------------------------------------------
GOLDEN RULES:

1. Correctness is King: Task Completion and Factual Accuracy are almost always more 
   important than AI Performance. A clumsy response that gets the right answer is 
   better than a graceful response that gets the wrong answer.

2. Performance is the Tie-Breaker: If both responses are correct and complete, use AI 
   Performance to decide which is "slightly better." The more efficient and skillful 
   agent wins.
